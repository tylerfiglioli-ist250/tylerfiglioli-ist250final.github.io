About Baltimore Country Club

Baltimore Country Club is one of the most historic and prestigious private clubs in the United States, located in the state of Maryland. Founded in 1898, it is widely recognized for its rich tradition in golf and country club culture. The club features two championship courses—the East Course in Five Farms and the West Course in Five Farms—both known for their challenging layouts and pristine conditions. Baltimore Country Club has hosted several major tournaments, including the 1928 PGA Championship, helping to cement its reputation in the golfing world. Beyond golf, the club offers a range of amenities such as tennis, swimming, and fine dining, creating a well-rounded experience for its members.
