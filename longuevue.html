About Longue Vue Club

Longue Vue Club is a historic and highly regarded private club located just outside Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Established in 1920, it has built a strong reputation for both its championship golf course and its welcoming social atmosphere. The course was originally designed by the legendary architect Donald Ross and later enhanced by Arnold Palmer, blending classic design elements with modern playability. Known for its tree-lined fairways and strategically placed hazards, the course offers a challenging yet enjoyable experience for golfers of all skill levels. In addition to golf, Longue Vue Club provides amenities such as tennis courts, swimming facilities, and dining options, making it a well-rounded destination for recreation and community engagement.
