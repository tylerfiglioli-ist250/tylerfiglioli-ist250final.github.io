About Allegheny Country Club

Allegheny Country Club is a prestigious private golf club located just outside Pittsburgh, known for its rich history and classic design. Founded in 1902, the club has long been recognized as one of the top golf destinations in Pennsylvania, featuring a championship course originally designed by renowned architect Donald Ross. The course is celebrated for its strategic layout, rolling terrain, and well-maintained greens that challenge golfers of all skill levels. Beyond golf, the club offers a range of upscale amenities, including dining facilities, social events, and recreational opportunities, making it a central hub for members seeking both competitive play and a refined social experience.
